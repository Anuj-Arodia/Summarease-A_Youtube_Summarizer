# SUMMAREASE
SummarEase – AI-Powered Text & Video Summarization Platform

SummarEase is an intelligent summarization platform designed to transform lengthy text, documents, and video transcripts into concise, meaningful, and easy-to-understand summaries. The project focuses on improving information accessibility, reducing reading time, and enhancing productivity using modern AI and NLP techniques.

🚀 Features:

📄 Text Summarization
Generate concise summaries from long articles, reports, notes, and documents.

🎥 Video Transcript Summarization
Convert video transcripts into short and informative summaries.

🤖 AI-Powered Processing
Uses Natural Language Processing (NLP) techniques for accurate content understanding.

⚡ Fast & Efficient
Quickly processes large amounts of content with optimized performance.

🎯 User-Friendly Interface
Clean and simple UI for seamless interaction.

📊 Smart Content Extraction
Identifies key points, important sentences, and relevant information automatically.

🔒 Scalable & Extensible
Easily customizable for future AI enhancements and integrations.
